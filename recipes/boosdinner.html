Recipe for Boos Dinner

What is Boos Dinner?

Boos Dinner is a marinated version of his hard kibble.

Ingredients.

  • 2oz of Purina Pro Plan Sensitive Skin and Stomach.
  • 2oz of Water
  • 1oz Rx Vitamins for Pets Liquid Nutricalm.

Directions.

  1. Place dry kibble in bowl.
  2. Pour water into bowl of kibble.
  3. Let it marinate for about an hour.
  4. Once kibble is soft, mix with spoon, so that kibble is not stuck together.
  5. Drizzle Liquid Nutricalm, and serve!
